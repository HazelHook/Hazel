## Why Choose Hazel?

With Hazel's integration, your webhook management becomes streamlined and centralized. Key benefits include:

- **Unified Access**: All your webhooks are managed and accessible in one place.
- **Advanced Features**: Unlock features like [automatic retries](/features/retries), [delays](/features/delays), [alerting](/features/alerting), and [centralized monitoring](/features/monitoring) on all of your webhooks instantly.
- **Local Testing**: Makes local testing and development simple with our [local client](/features/local).
- **Enhanced Capabilities**: Hazel acts as a middleware, augmenting your webhooks' functionality and unifying your infrastructure for improved performance and reliability.
- **Never miss a webhook again**: Hazel manages all your webhooks and makes sure you will never miss a webhook and will make sure to keep you updated if something goes wrong delivering your webhook making debugging painless as ever.

## Key Features of Hazel:

- **Advanced Monitoring and Alerting**: Real-time monitoring and customizable alerting to maintain high performance and quickly address issues.
- **Reliability at Scale**: Engineered for high-volume webhook processing, ensuring consistent and reliable performance.
- **Intuitive Dashboard**: Centralized management of webhook activity, with detailed analytics and historical data access.
- **Developer-Friendly Integration**: Streamlined API and local client for efficient development, testing, and deployment workflows.
